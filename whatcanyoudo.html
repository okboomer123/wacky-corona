What can you do?

There is many things you can do while social distancing. You can do excersize. You can walk your dog(If you have one). You can play a online game with your friends. You can clean your house. You can work from home(if you have a job and are able to). You can learn how to cook. You can learn in general. Basically what I am saying is that there is many things you can do instead of just sitting in your home panicking wondering what could go wrong.
